CPT, ICD-10 & Billing Q&A

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Revenue Cycle Management?

Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) covers all financial activities from patient registration to final payment collection.

What is AR Follow-Up?

AR Follow-Up means actively reviewing unpaid claims and coordinating with payers to resolve delays and secure payment.

What causes claim denials?

Common reasons include coding errors, missing documentation, eligibility issues, authorization gaps, and filing deadline misses.

What is the difference between CPT and ICD-10?

CPT codes represent services/procedures performed, while ICD-10 codes represent diagnoses and clinical conditions.
