        <div class="faq-list">
          <details>
            <summary>What is Revenue Cycle Management?</summary>
            <p>
              Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) is the complete financial process from appointment scheduling
              and registration to final payment collection and account closure.
            </p>
          </details>

          <details>
            <summary>What is AR Follow-Up?</summary>
            <p>
              AR Follow-Up is the process of tracking unpaid claims, contacting payers, correcting issues,
              and securing pending reimbursements.
            </p>
          </details>

          <details>
            <summary>What causes claim denials?</summary>
            <p>
              Frequent causes include coding mismatches, missing documentation, late filing, eligibility gaps,
              and authorization errors.
            </p>
          </details>

          <details>
            <summary>What is the difference between CPT and ICD-10?</summary>
            <p>
              CPT defines the service performed, while ICD-10 identifies the diagnosis that supports why the
              service was medically necessary.
            </p>
          </details>
